About Our Site

Welcome to Moist Weather, your moist source for accurate and real-time weather information. Our site is designed to provide you with a sleek, interactive, and reliable way to check the weather anywhere in the world.

Key Features:

  • Real-Time Data: We use a powerful weather API to provide up-to-the-minute weather conditions, temperature, humidity, and more.
  • Global Coverage: Search for any city to get instant weather updates for your location or any other.
  • Detailed Forecasts: Get an hourly and daily breakdown of upcoming weather to plan your day with confidence.
  • Time-of-Day Insights: Our app adjusts to show the current time of day—be it Dawn, Day, Afternoon, or Night—for a more personalized experience.

This site was built using a Node.js backend with Express.js and a frontend powered by JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. We are committed to providing you with the moist reliable and user-friendly weather experience.
